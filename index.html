Background

Have you ever wondered which of San Francisco or Los Angeles is a busier airport? Or Have you experienced significant flight delay that made you miss important events? Understanding the curiosity of aviation fans and pain points of frequent flyers who are sensitive to flight delay, we want to create this interactive website for them to make better flight choices to know about flight traffic and avoid flight delay as much as possible.

TRAFFIC

Flight Route Map across USA

In 2015, all 12 US airlines carried out a total of 5.8 million flights between 628 airports. Chicago ranked No.1 among cities and Southwest ranked No.1 among airlines. The current map below shows all flights from Chicago with Southwest Airline.

Choose Departure City or Airline on the right dropdown filters and interact with the map!

  • By choosing a city, the map shows flights departing from that city, and you can futher filter those flights by airlines.
  • By choosing an airline and choosing 'All' for cities, you can view all flights for the airline and know its hubs.

Delay in USA

Point-to-Point Delay Around USA

Select the origin cities and destination cities on the top right to filter the cities you are interested in. Hover on the map's routes, get to know the delay between different cities.

Flight delays over a year of each airline

In general, all airlines witness heavy delays during first weeks of January and last weeks of December , and the least delays at end of April to May. Click on the start button to view delay information for each airline. The circle size represents their flight traffic for that day. You can mouseover the date to move forward and backwards through time.

Delay by Time

To get an overview of which airlines perform badly on being on time, select the airlines on the top right filters to compare airlines delay time within one week and within one year.
