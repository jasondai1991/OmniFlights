Flight Route Map across USA

In 2015, all 12 US airlines carried out a totl of 5.8 million flights from 628 original airports to 629 destination airports all over USA. Among all cities, Chicago ranked No.1 and flew out over 430,000 flights, while San Francisco ranked No.8. For all airlines, Southwest was No.1 and had over 1.4 million flights with its hub in Chicago, Las Vegas and Baltimore. Enjoy your time and explore the chart below: Flight Route Map across USA to view flight traffic from a certain city, of an airline or view detailed flight information between any two points.

Average Flight Traffic every 10 minutes

Do you know that in 2015, there were 113 flights taking off every 10 minutes in average within USA? And the peak happened in the early morning at 6:00, yes with nearly 360 flights taking off between 6:00 and 6:10 . The next peak is at 7:00 with a little over 300 flights taking off in the next 10 minutes. Speaking of individual cities, Atlanta has a surprising 25.5 flights taking off between 13:40 and 13:50 and that is the most among all cities. Living in San Francisco for many years and not knowing that peak hours of the San Francisco Airport is around 10:40 ? Always curious to find out which one of SF or LA has more traffic? Our chart tells you that the winner is LA. Want to explore more of traffic for different airlines, and even compare cities under each airline?
